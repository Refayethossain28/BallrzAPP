How to play 🤫

One phone, passed around the table. Everyone shares a single secret word — everyone except the imposter, who's flying blind.

  1. Deal. Pass the phone. Each player privately reveals their card — the secret word, or "you're the imposter".
  2. Clues. In the order shown, each player says one word linked to the secret. Prove you know it without giving it away. The imposter has to fake it.
  3. Discuss. Argue. Who was vague? Who was suspiciously perfect?
  4. Vote. Agree on one suspect and accuse.
  5. Reveal. Caught the imposter? The crew scores — unless the imposter can name the secret word and steal it. Accuse an innocent and the imposter walks.

Scoring

Crew catch the imposter: +1 each. Imposter evades or you mis-accuse: +2 imposter. Caught imposter names the word: +3 imposter (steal).

Two ways to play

🔤 Word — everyone shares one secret word; the imposter has none (or a decoy: a different word from the same category, so they may not even know they're wrong).

📍 Locations — everyone gets the same place and a character role to act out; the spy gets neither and has to deduce where they are. Caught? The spy can still steal it by naming the location.

Match play

Set Play to a target and the first to reach it takes the crown, with a champion screen, MVP and confetti. Turn on Two clue rounds for a longer read on everyone. Build your own Custom category, or flip on Live AI to have Claude generate one from any theme.

Tips

A clue too obvious helps the imposter blend. Too obscure and your own team suspects you. Live in the middle.

About

A zero-build, offline-first party game. No account, no servers — every card, vote and score lives only on this device. Part of the BallrzAPP prototypes.

Live AI runs through a zero-dependency proxy that keeps your key server-side — ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=sk-ant-… node imposter/server.mjs, then open it from http://localhost:8790.

Custom category

Make your own word pack — inside jokes, your friend group, a TV show. One word or short phrase per line (10+ is best).
