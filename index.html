BallrzAPP — runnable prototypes

A handful of app concepts, each shipped as a zero-build prototype you can open and poke.

⭐ Imposter · party game
The best pass-and-play imposter game
One phone, 3–12 players, no account. Everyone shares a secret word — everyone except the imposter, bluffing blind. Pass the phone to privately reveal your card, give a one-word clue in turn, run the discussion timer, then vote. Unmask them and the crew scores — unless they name the word and steal it at the buzzer. Now with a whole second mode — 📍 Locations (Spyfall-style), where everyone gets a place and a character role and the spy must deduce where they are — plus match play to a target score with a champion screen, MVP & confetti, persistent per-player stats, a custom pack editor, optional ✦ Live AI categories (Claude via a zero-dependency proxy), two-clue-round mode, 15 word packs, decoy mode, share-results, sound, haptics & hold-to-reveal. Installable PWA, fully offline, tested deal/vote/score/stats engine.
⭐ Ripple · private messenger
A messenger that puts you in control
Like WhatsApp — but you can schedule messages for later, edit and unsend them, set a chat to disappear, run inline polls, fire slash commands (/poll, /remind, /expire), record voice notes, and search every chat instantly. Reactions, replies, read receipts, typing, pin/mute/archive. Optional App Lock encrypts everything on-device (AES-GCM). Chat with the built-in Echo bot, or open two tabs to message yourself live — no account needed to try it. Real cross-device messaging is built in: add a Firebase config in ripple/config.js and people join a chat from a shared invite link (Anonymous Auth + Firestore, member-scoped rules). Installable PWA, offline-first, unit-tested logic (SETUP.md).
⭐ Cusp · what to do right now
The decision engine for your to-do list
Every other app lists your tasks. Cusp decides: of everything on your plate, what should you do right now? The on-device Salience Engine scores each task on importance, deadline tightness, how its mental load fits your current energy, whether it fits the minutes you actually have free, and your momentum — then shows its working so the pick is yours to trust. Focus timer, right-now plan, dependency-aware, anti-rot. No account, offline, installable PWA.
⭐ Omni · do-everything app
One home for your whole day
Tasks & calendar, notes, calculator, unit & currency converter, timers/pomodoro, habits, money log, weather, text tools, password generator, QR codes, world clock — all in one installable, offline-first PWA. No account, no cloud. ⌘K to jump anywhere.
⭐ My Own AI Model · GPT from scratch
A language model that's genuinely yours
Not an API wrapper — a real GPT built from first principles: a hand-written autograd engine, a transformer with causal self-attention, and an Adam trainer, all in pure NumPy with nothing hidden behind a framework. It was trained offline, its weights baked into the page, and the forward pass re-implemented in JavaScript — so every token is generated on your device. Type a prompt, set temperature / top-k / length, and watch it stream token by token. No server, no API, no cloud. Full training code in llm-from-scratch/. Installable PWA, fully offline.
Lingua · learn & translate
Any language — and its dialects
Pick from 90+ languages, then translate or get a teaching lesson. Real dialect support — Arabic Fusha, Egyptian, Saudi, Emirati (and more) and Urdu Lahori, Karachi, Dakhini (and more). Works offline with a starter phrasebook; flip on Live AI (Claude via a zero-dependency local proxy, key stays server-side) for full-accuracy translations and lessons. Installable PWA.
Apex · UK Lettings
Landlord ↔ renter marketplace
Landlords advertise property (real listing photos, with an offline illustration fallback), renters search & enquire. Message, book a viewing, agree terms, then both parties sign a UK-compliant Assured Shorthold Tenancy. The landlord pays £100 on signing. Toggle Renter/Landlord to play both sides. Installable PWA with an Apex splash screen.
