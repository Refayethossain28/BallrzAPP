BallrzAPP — runnable prototypes

A handful of app concepts, each shipped as a zero-build prototype you can open and poke.

⭐ Omni · do-everything app
One home for your whole day
Tasks & calendar, notes, calculator, unit & currency converter, timers/pomodoro, habits, money log, weather, text tools, password generator, QR codes, world clock — all in one installable, offline-first PWA. No account, no cloud. ⌘K to jump anywhere.
ApexVIP · Luxury Chauffeur
Client app
Book a chauffeur, track your driver live, and message them in-app. Installable as an iPhone home-screen app.
ApexVIP · Driver
Driver portal
Accept & manage jobs, live GPS, earnings.
ApexVIP · Admin
Operations panel
Dispatch, fleet, analytics & payouts.
Concept 1 · AI Life Concierge
Split-the-bill agent
Chat → the agent proposes a split → you confirm → idempotent payment requests. Runs offline in stub mode; toggle “Live” to use Claude tool-use via the proxy.
Concept 2 · AI Infinite Game
Flow-state loop
On-device skill model + an offline level pool + flow-band difficulty selection. The engine internals are surfaced so you can watch it adapt to you.
