⭐ Imposter · party game
The best pass-and-play imposter game
One phone, 3–12 players, no account. Everyone shares a secret word — everyone except the imposter, bluffing blind. Pass the phone to privately reveal your card, give a one-word clue in turn, run the discussion timer, then vote. Unmask them and the crew scores — unless they name the word and steal it at the buzzer. Now with a whole second mode — 📍 Locations (Spyfall-style), where everyone gets a place and a character role and the spy must deduce where they are — plus match play to a target score with a champion screen, MVP & confetti, persistent per-player stats, a custom pack editor, optional ✦ Live AI categories (Claude via a zero-dependency proxy), two-clue-round mode, 15 word packs, decoy mode, share-results, sound, haptics & hold-to-reveal. Installable PWA, fully offline, tested deal/vote/score/stats engine.
⭐ Cortex · the daily brain gym
The mental exercise game every human needs
Five adaptive drills, five cognitive domains: 🧩 Flashback (grid memory), ➗ Number Storm (rapid mental arithmetic), 🎯 Ink Trap (the classic Stroop test — answer the ink, not the word), 🔁 Echo (a vanishing symbol stream held in working memory) and ⚡ Odd One Out (visual search). Every round rides a psychophysical staircase — hit → harder, miss → easier — so the game always sits at your edge, the zone that actually trains. Speed points, a combo multiplier, per-domain ratings that roll up into your Brain Index and rank (Spark → Limitless), a 🔥 daily streak, and a daily workout — the same 3-drill circuit dealt to everyone on Earth each day. Deterministic, seeded, unit-tested Drill Engine; no account, fully offline, installable PWA.
⭐ ApexVIP · Concierge
One membership. Everything handled.
The all-in-one VIP concierge, as a subscription: pick Silver £49 / Gold £199 / Black £499 a month (7-day free trial), then ask your concierge for anything across 8 desks — travel, dining, events, chauffeur, personal shopping, home & errands, wellness, gifting. Every request runs a real lifecycle — received → with your concierge → sourcing → three priced options → confirmed → completed — in a live chat thread, with tier-based SLAs (Black gets a 15-minute first response and jumps every queue), monthly request quotas, prorated upgrades, scheduled downgrades, cancel-at-period-end, billing history, and ApexVIP points & status (Member → Insider → Icon → Legend). All the money logic lives in a deterministic, unit-tested Membership Engine, and the full cloud backend is built in: real accounts, live cross-device sync over Firestore, and real payments via Stripe Billing (Checkout + Billing Portal + webhook — see concierge/SETUP.md). Works with no account, offline, installable PWA.
⭐ Ripple · private messenger
A messenger that puts you in control
Like WhatsApp — but you can schedule messages for later, edit and unsend them, set a chat to disappear, run inline polls, fire slash commands (/poll, /remind, /expire), record voice notes, and search every chat instantly. Reactions, replies, read receipts, typing, pin/mute/archive. Optional App Lock encrypts everything on-device (AES-GCM). Chat with the built-in Echo bot, or open two tabs to message yourself live — no account needed to try it. Real cross-device messaging is built in: add a Firebase config in ripple/config.js and people join a chat from a shared invite link (Anonymous Auth + Firestore, member-scoped rules). Installable PWA, offline-first, unit-tested logic (SETUP.md).
🎴 Intro · digital business card
Your paper card, retired
Design a digital business card that lives on your phone — name, role, photo, contacts, socials, and 8 card themes with a live preview. Hand it over as a link or QR code: the whole card travels inside the link itself, so there's no server, no database, no account — for you or for them. The receiver taps Save to contacts and gets a proper vCard (photo, number and links intact), or keeps you in their in-app wallet. A built-in 📷 QR scanner pulls someone else's card straight into yours, and 📳 NFC tap writes your card onto any cheap NFC tag (Chrome/Android) so a tap opens it on any phone — Popl-style, but yours. 🎟 Apple Wallet included: a zero-dependency signed-.pkpass pipeline — bring your Apple Developer cert and your card lives in Wallet. Multiple cards (work / side-hustle / personal), on-device photo resizing, and every serious bit — the share codec, the vCard generator, the from-scratch QR encoder — is a pure, deterministic, unit-tested engine. Offline, installable PWA.
💧 Drip · the passive income engine
Design the machine that pays you while you sleep
Every real passive income stream is the same machine: capital → income → back into capital. Model yours — 🏦 savings, 📈 dividends, 🏠 rental, 🎼 royalties, 💾 products — and the deterministic engine runs the compounding month by month: what the drip pays now, what it pays in 10–30 years (inflation-adjusted too), the % of your bills already covered, and your 🏁 freedom date — the month passive income covers your expenses — drawn on the chart. Honest passivity and diversification scores included. A planner, not a promise: your assumptions, unit-tested arithmetic. No account, offline, installable PWA.

⭐ My Own AI Model · GPT from scratch
A language model that's genuinely yours
Not an API wrapper — a real GPT built from first principles: a hand-written autograd engine, a transformer with causal self-attention, and an Adam trainer, all in pure NumPy with nothing hidden behind a framework. It was trained offline, its weights baked into the page, and the forward pass re-implemented in JavaScript — so every token is generated on your device. Type a prompt and watch it stream token by token, generated on your device — no server, no API, no cloud. Trained on tiny-Shakespeare with a from-scratch BPE tokenizer, so it reads with real words and cadence (but it's a tiny stylistic echo, not a smart assistant). Want real intelligence in the same app? Flip on ⚡ Live AI to stream from real Fable 5 and compare (needs the local proxy). Full code in llm-from-scratch/. Installable PWA, fully offline.
⭐ Neura · the AI cryptocurrency
The chain that thinks — a store of value with a mind
An AI-native cryptocurrency built on the same from-scratch Bitcoin engine as TimeCoin, plus one new consensus rule: Proof of Intelligence. Every block must advance the chain's shared neural network by one exact training step (seeded by the previous block's hash) and commit the weights' hash under the proof-of-work — every node re-runs the training to verify it, so the learning can't be faked, skipped or cherry-picked. The network is learning to paint its own mark: watch the mind of the chain sharpen with every block you mine. Hard-capped at 21,000,000 NEURA — Bitcoin's exact halving curve, no premine, genesis pays nobody — designed as a store of value whose security budget also grows a mind the whole network co-owns. Wallets, mining, sending, a block explorer, and multi-tab node sync, all in one file (npm run test:neura — 17 tests). A working prototype and an honest one — not an investment.
⭐ BallrzCoin · my own cryptocurrency
A Bitcoin built from raw bytes up
A complete proof-of-work cryptocurrency with zero dependencies — the same building blocks as Bitcoin, implemented from their specs: SHA-256, ECDSA on secp256k1 (Bitcoin's exact curve, RFC 6979 deterministic signatures), base58check addresses, a UTXO ledger, merkle trees, mining with difficulty retargeting, a reward halving toward a hard cap — 21 billion BLZ, fixed forever, sized for a worldwide community that trades it for time and favours — fees to the miner, and fork choice by cumulative work. Create wallets, ⛏ mine real blocks in your browser, send coins, watch the mempool and block explorer — then open a second tab: each tab is a node, syncing on the heaviest chain like a real network. Crypto verified against published test vectors; consensus rules unit-tested against double-spends, forged signatures and doctored forks (npm run test:coin — 39 tests). A real community currency for time and favours — no ICO, no price, not an investment.
