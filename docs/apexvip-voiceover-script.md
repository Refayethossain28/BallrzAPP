# ApexVIP — Documentary Voiceover Script

**Format:** ~2:30 product film · single narrator (warm, composed, British) ·
ambient piano + low pulse under. Timecodes are guides. `[VISUAL]` = on-screen;
`VO:` = narration. Grounded entirely in the shipped product.

---

### 0:00 — Cold open  *(0:00–0:14)*
`[VISUAL]` Black. A single gold line draws the ApexVIP wordmark. Night Mayfair
through a rain-flecked window; an S-Class pulls up. The splash video.

> **VO:** "London moves fast. The people who matter move differently."

`[SFX]` Door closes. Music swells gently.

---

### 0:14 — Positioning  *(0:14–0:30)*
`[VISUAL]` The three apps fan out on screen — passenger, driver, operator.

> **VO:** "ApexVIP isn't a taxi app. It's a chauffeur and a concierge — three
> connected apps, one intelligent system. Beyond the journey."

---

### 0:30 — The passenger  *(0:30–0:54)*
`[VISUAL]` Thumb scrolls the passenger app: Airport, By the Hour, By the Day,
Point-to-Point. A hotel card with a live nightly rate. A curated experience.

> **VO:** "For the guest, it's effortless. Book an airport transfer, a car by the
> hour, or a private day. Discover London's finest hotels — with live rates — and
> the experiences worth leaving home for. One price, VAT included, no surge, no
> surprises."

---

### 0:54 — ApexAI  *(0:54–1:14)*
`[VISUAL]` The concierge chat. The guest types: *"Collect me from Mayfair tomorrow
at 9 for BA247."* The fields fill themselves; a confirmed booking slides up.

> **VO:** "And at its centre — ApexAI. Don't fill in a form. Just say what you need.
> ApexAI understands the request, watches the flight, and has the car ready before
> you ask. Nobody else in this class does that."

---

### 1:14 — The driver  *(1:14–1:34)*
`[VISUAL]` Driver app: go online, a job arrives, one-tap claim, guided trip steps.
Then an earnings figure ticking up; a "Payouts · active" badge.

> **VO:** "For the chauffeur, it's just as clean. Go online. Claim the job. Drive.
> Eighty percent of every fare — paid straight to your bank."

---

### 1:34 — Trust & the operator  *(1:34–1:58)*
`[VISUAL]` Operator console: live map, the dispatch board. A compliance panel —
licence, PCO, insurance, DBS — each turning green. A red "Expired" flag auto-takes
a driver off-duty. The audit log scrolls.

> **VO:** "Behind it all, the control room. Every driver vetted — licence, DBS,
> insurance, the vehicle's MOT — and the moment one lapses, they're off the road,
> automatically. Every action recorded, and never erased. This is what a licensed
> operator actually has to prove — built in."

---

### 1:58 — Quick Intake  *(1:58–2:08)*
`[VISUAL]` Operator pastes a text message into Quick Intake; ApexAI parses it; the
booking lands on the board and broadcasts to drivers.

> **VO:** "A booking by phone? Paste it in. ApexAI turns words into a dispatched car
> in seconds."

---

### 2:08 — The comparison  *(2:08–2:24)*
`[VISUAL]` A clean table animates in: ApexVIP beside Wheely, Blacklane, Addison Lee,
Uber. Green ticks march down the ApexVIP column — AI concierge, hotels, compliance,
payouts, audit.

> **VO:** "The names you know have the cars on the road. ApexVIP has the better
> system — an AI concierge, a lifestyle layer, compliance and payouts built in."

---

### 2:24 — Close  *(2:24–2:34)*
`[VISUAL]` Back to the gold wordmark on black. Tagline fades up.

> **VO:** "ApexVIP. Beyond the journey."

`[SFX]` Final piano note. Cut to black.

---

## Production notes
- **Runtime:** ~2:34. For a 60-second cut, keep Cold Open, ApexAI, Compliance,
  Close.
- **Honesty guardrail:** the film shows *software that exists*. Don't imply a live
  fleet or operating history — the licensing/insurance/go-live steps are still
  ahead (see `apexvip-launch-runbook.md`). Frame as "built to launch," not "live."
- **Screen capture:** record the real PWAs (client/driver/admin) on device; use the
  splash video (`splash-bg.mp4`) for the open/close.
- **Tone:** restrained luxury. Let silence and the product breathe; the VO under-sells.
