# ApexVIP
### Beyond the journey.

Luxury chauffeur **+** lifestyle concierge — three apps, one AI.

---

## The problem

London's premium-mobility apps move you from **A to B**.

None of them answer *"where should I stay"* or *"find me a table"* —
and none let you **book by simply talking**.

---

## What it is

**One intelligent system, three connected apps:**

🧍 **Passenger** · 🚗 **Driver** · 🎛 **Operator**

…with **ApexAI** — a natural-language concierge — at the centre.

---

## For the passenger

- Airport · By the Hour · By the Day · Point-to-Point
- **Hotels with live nightly rates** + curated experiences
- Talk to **ApexAI**: *"Car to Heathrow T5 tomorrow 9am, BA247"*
- Apple / Google Pay · flight-aware pickups · live tracking
- **VAT-inclusive pricing** — no surge, no surprises

---

## ApexAI

> *"Collect me from Mayfair tomorrow at 9 for BA247."*

A Claude-powered concierge turns a sentence into a **confirmed booking** —
with an on-device fallback so it never hard-fails.

**No forms. Just words.**

---

## For the driver

- Go online → **claim a job** → guided trip
- Upload licence / PCO / insurance / DBS / V5C / badge
- **80% of every fare**, paid to your bank via Stripe Connect
- Earnings + ApexCoin tracked in real time

---

## For the operator

- Live bookings, map & **dispatch board**
- **Driver compliance** — vetting + automatic **expiry enforcement**
- Vehicle MOT / road-tax tracking
- Pricing · analytics · **payouts** · **immutable audit log**
- **⚡ Quick Intake** — paste a booking, ApexAI dispatches it

---

## Trust, built in

- A lapsed DBS or MOT takes a driver **off-duty within 24h** — automatically
- Every operator action **recorded and tamper-proof**
- Security rules block self-approval & role self-promotion

*Exactly what a TfL-licensed operator must evidence.*
