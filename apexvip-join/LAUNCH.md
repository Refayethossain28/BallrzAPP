## The one principle

You're not selling a coin or hyping a token — you're testing whether people
want a concierge. So the ask is soft: *"Would you use this? Reserve a spot,
it's free."* Signups = demand. No signups = you learned it cheaply. Post, then
watch the Formspree inbox.

## After you post — what to watch

1. **Which tier do signups pick?** (Formspree captures it.) That's your real
   price signal — if everyone picks Silver, £499 Black may be too rich; if
   people reach for Black, you've found premium demand.
2. **Where did they come from?** Post to a few channels and note which one
   converts — that's where your customers actually are.
3. **Reply to every early signup personally.** Ask what they'd want handled
   first. Those conversations are worth more than any analytics.

When signups are real, come back and switch on the billing that's already
built ([`../concierge/SETUP.md`](../concierge/SETUP.md)) and start serving your
founders by hand.
