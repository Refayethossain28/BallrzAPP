# BallrzCoin promo kit

Shareable material for showing off the coin. The pitch is the honest one — it's
also the strongest one: *a real Bitcoin-style blockchain, radically scarce, and
proudly a toy.* No investment language, ever: BLZ has no price and is not for
sale — that's a feature, and it keeps you on the right side of the law.

## Assets

| File | Use |
| --- | --- |
| [`share-card.png`](share-card.png) (1200×630 @2x) | Link previews, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, blogs. Also wired into the page's OG tags, so **just pasting the coin URL into WhatsApp/iMessage/Slack/X shows this card automatically.** |
| [`share-square.png`](share-square.png) (1080×1080 @2x) | Instagram, WhatsApp status, anywhere square. |

Both cards carry a scannable QR (generated by the coin's own encoder) that opens
the app.

## Regenerating the cards

The cards are rendered from HTML by a script in the session that produced them;
tweak `coin/index.html`'s OG tags if you move the images. Keep the honesty
line — *"No ICO. No price. No promises."* — on anything you publish: it's what
makes this shareable without being mistaken for a token sale.
