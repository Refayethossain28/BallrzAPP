What it is

A complete proof-of-work cryptocurrency, built from the same building blocks as Bitcoin — real cryptography, a shared ledger no one can secretly edit, and a fixed supply of 21 billion BLZ, forever. But where Bitcoin became a speculative asset, BallrzCoin is built to be used: a digital time bank where one coin is worth an hour of a favour, and your community agrees what everything costs.

Its purpose

To let a group of people — friends, a neighbourhood, a co-op, a whole town — trade time and favours without needing a bank or cash. Bake a cake, fix a bike, mind the kids, give a lift: contribute, earn BLZ, and spend it on what you need back. It turns goodwill into a currency your circle can actually run on — the way babysitting co-ops and local exchange schemes have worked for decades, now with a tamper-proof digital ledger.

An alternative to banks & cash

🏦

Instead of a bank

No account to open, no ID check, no fees, no minimum balance, no one who can freeze or close it. BallrzCoin does what a bank does — hold and move value — but you are the bank.

💵

Instead of cash

Like cash it’s peer-to-peer and needs no middleman — but it travels any distance instantly, can’t be pickpocketed, and comes back from a recovery phrase if you lose your phone.

Advantages over the banking system

💸

No fees, no skim

Banks profit on every account and transfer. Here the network is the users — value moves for free.

🔑

You hold your own money

Self-custody: your keys, your coins. No institution can freeze, seize, or close your account.

🌍

Open to everyone

No bank account, ID or credit check. ~1.4 billion adults are unbanked — this needs only a phone.

🚫

No gatekeeper

No one decides whether your payment is “allowed.” If two people agree, it happens.

🔎

Fully transparent

Every rule and every transaction is open and auditable — no hidden ledger, no fine print.

🪙

Sound & scarce

21 billion BLZ, fixed forever. No one can print more and quietly erode what you hold.

The honest part. BallrzCoin is real, but it’s not a bank and not an investment. There’s no cash price and nothing to buy — its worth is simply what your community agrees a coin buys in time and favours. It has no deposit insurance, it’s still early, and its security is being hardened in the open (see SECURITY.md). Keep it to favours and goods, not cash — that’s what keeps it a community currency rather than a financial product. Use it for what it’s brilliant at: letting people help each other without a bank in the way.
