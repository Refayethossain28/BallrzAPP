Run BallrzCoin barter
with your friends 🤝

BallrzCoin is a real cryptocurrency, but it isn’t money — it’s a shared token you and your circle can trade for favours and small things. Here’s how to get a little barter economy going in about two minutes.

The idea in one line

Everyone earns BLZ by doing things for others, and spends it getting things back. It works because you all agree it’s worth something — nothing more, nothing less.

Get set up

1

Everyone opens the same link

Share your network link (the ballrzcoin.onrender.com address, or the invite link from the app’s Network panel). Each person who opens it joins the same shared economy. Add it to the home screen so it feels like an app.

2

Agree your price list together

Open the 💡 Suggested price list on the barter board and, as a group, decide what things are worth to you. This is the most important step — the agreement is what gives BLZ value. A simple starting point:

☕ Coffee 1🚗 Lift 2🍳 Meal 3🧹 An hour’s help 2🎂 Cake 3📦 Help you move 8
3

Everyone gets some starting coins

Coins come from mining (tap ⛏ Mine to this wallet). If you (the host) mined the early coins, be generous — send each friend a few BLZ to start so nobody’s stuck at zero. A fair start makes the whole thing fun.

4

Post what you can offer

On the Barter — offers board, post things you’ll do or give (“🎂 Bake a cake — 3 BLZ”). Everyone on the network sees it. Tap a suggested-price chip to fill it in fast.

5

Pay each other — and confirm

Tap Pay on someone’s offer (or scan their QR) to fill in a payment, hit Sign & broadcast, then mine one block to confirm it. That last step seals the payment into the blockchain — it’s not final until a block is mined.

The golden rules

💛 It’s worth what you agree. BLZ has no cash price and never will. Its value is your group’s honour system — keep it that way.
🤝 Favours & goods, not cash. Trade coffees, lifts, cakes and help. The moment you swap BLZ for real money, it stops being a community favours currency and becomes a legal/tax matter — don’t.
🎁 Keep newcomers funded. Send a few BLZ to anyone who joins so they can start trading straight away.
😄 Have fun with it. It’s a game that happens to teach you exactly how Bitcoin works. Race the leaderboard, tip generously, don’t take it too seriously.

Quick reference

To get paid

  • Tap your wallet’s ▦ QR and let them scan it, or
  • Tap your address to copy it and send it over.

To pay someone

  • Tap Pay on their offer, or 📷 Scan their QR, or paste their address into Send coins.
  • Enter the amount → Sign & broadcast → ⛏ Mine a block to confirm.

To never lose your coins

  • Tap 🔑 Keys → 🖨 Paper wallet and save it. That’s your backup — anyone with the private key owns the coins, so keep it safe.
