⛏ Miner

In BallrzCoin, wallets hold and spend; miners do the work. This page does the proof-of-work that confirms your circle's transactions and issues new coins — the job a wallet deliberately can't do. Point it at a wallet address, press start, and keep the tab open: while it runs, payments in your circle confirm and the block reward flows to that address. One miner is enough to serve a whole circle sharing a relay.
Idle — set a payout address and press start.
