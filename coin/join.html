A neighbourhood favours circle

Swap favours,
not cash.

Help each other out — a lift into town, a cooked meal, a leaky tap, a hand moving a sofa — and keep score in hours, not money. Everyone’s hour counts the same.

How it works
  1. Join, free
    Thirty seconds, nothing to download. You start with a couple of hours to spend.
  2. Help someone
    Do a favour from the board — you earn that many hours.
  3. Spend your hours
    Need a hand yourself? Someone in the circle helps, and you pay in hours.
Why people love it
  • It’s fair. An hour of your time is worth an hour of anyone else’s — no haggling.
  • No money needed. No fees, no bank, nobody taking a cut.
  • It builds community. You get to know — and rely on — the people around you.
  • It’s genuinely yours. Nothing to buy, nothing to sell, no catch.
