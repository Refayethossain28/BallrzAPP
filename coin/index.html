🪙 BallrzCoin BLZ

A toy proof-of-work cryptocurrency built like Bitcoin: secp256k1 wallets, UTXO transactions, merkle trees, mining with difficulty retargeting and halving rewards — but only 21 BLZ will ever exist, a million times scarcer than Bitcoin's 21,000,000. Open this page in a second tab — each tab is a node, and they sync on the heaviest chain.

Barter — offers board

Post something you’ll do or give for BLZ, and pay others for theirs. Offers travel the network like payments. BLZ is only worth what your group agrees it is — so agree a shared price list (a coffee = 1 BLZ, a lift = 2, a home-cooked meal = 3…).
