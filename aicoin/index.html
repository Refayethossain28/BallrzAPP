🧠 Spend AIT on inference — prompt-commitment receipts

Pay for AI by burning sparks to your prompt's commitment address — base58check(sha256d(prompt)[0..20]). No key exists for that address, so the payment is provably unspendable, commits to your exact prompt without revealing it, and shrinks the AIT supply. Any provider can verify the receipt from the prompt + txId alone.
