🧠 AI desk — spend sparks on real inference

Pay for AI with an on-chain receipt: the payment carries a burned commitment to sha256d(prompt), so it proves what was bought without revealing it. Leave the provider blank to burn the whole cost (deflationary, provider-neutral) or name a provider's address to pay them, with a 1-spark burn binding payment↔prompt. Once the receipt confirms, Run it: the from-scratch GPT (llm-from-scratch) generates on this device, token by token, stopping at exactly what you paid for.
