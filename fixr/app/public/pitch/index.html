Questions

FAQ

Do I have to change how my clients contact me?

No. Clients keep texting, calling, or emailing you. Your dispatcher pastes the message into Fixr and the AI turns it into a booking — you meet clients where they already are.

Who processes the payments?

Your own Stripe account. Fixr never becomes your processor and never takes a per-ride cut of the fare — you keep it. Fixr charges a flat monthly fee plus a small platform fee on payments processed.

How do drivers get paid?

When a trip is completed, the fare is captured and the driver's share is transferred to their connected Stripe account automatically. Drivers onboard for payouts in a couple of taps.

Is the concierge side real or coming later?

Real and live. A passenger can submit a concierge request (dining, tickets, a jet), and the operator prices and fulfills it on the same board as a ride.

Does the AI need setup?

No. It works out of the box. If the AI is ever unavailable, Fixr falls back to a rule-based parser so intake never breaks.

Can it handle more than one operator / scale?

Yes. It runs on a real database (Postgres) and is built to run many operators. Start with one; grow without re-platforming.
