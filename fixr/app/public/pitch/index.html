The problem

The industry runs on duct tape

Bookings by hand

Dispatchers retype messy calls and texts into dated software — slow, error-prone, double-bookings.

No system of record

Trips and client history live in WhatsApp and someone's head — and walk out the door when staff leave.

Payment friction

Forced processors, payment holds, and per-ride rakes eat margin and slow driver payouts.

Fragmented concierge

VIP requests — dinners, jets, tickets — get handled off-system, with no record or billing.
