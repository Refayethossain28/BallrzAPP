What it does

Everything a landlord actually does — automated.

Apex isn't a spreadsheet with a login. Money and legal state are server-authoritative, so the app enforces the rules instead of trusting the form.

Let & renew

Advertise, agree and sign — without leaving the app.

A let is a sequence with real legal and money steps. Apex drives all of it and only charges when it completes.

Enquiry

A renter finds the listing and messages the landlord in a shared deal room.

Viewing

Propose, confirm and track viewings — no email tag, no lost threads.

Agree

Both parties agree to proceed; the app checks the property is compliant to let.

Sign

A compliant AST is drafted from the kernel and e-signed by both sides.

Completed

Tenancy goes live and books into Rent automatically.

£100 fee · once, on completion
