Neura Ⓝ

The chain that thinks. A fixed 21,000,000 coins. A neural network that every block must train, and every node verifies. No premine, no committee, no printer — scarcity you can count and a mind you can watch grow.

🧠 Proof of Intelligence

Every block must advance the chain's shared neural network by one exact training step, seeded by the previous block's hash. The weights' hash is sealed under the proof-of-work — every node re-runs the training to verify it. Learning can't be faked, skipped or cherry-picked.

🪙 Absolute scarcity

Bitcoin's exact emission curve: a 50 NEURA subsidy halving every 210,000 blocks toward a hard cap of 21,000,000 — a thousand times scarcer than TimeCoin. The genesis block pays nobody; every coin is mined into existence.

🤝 A mind you co-own

The network's mind is consensus state: mine a single block and your training step is part of its intelligence forever. Holding NEURA is holding a share of everything the chain has learned.

Why be part of this

The store-of-value case, in three sentences.

Money keeps its value when nobody can print more of it — Neura's 21,000,000-coin cap is enforced by every node's consensus rules, not by anyone's promise. Proof of Intelligence means the energy that secures the ledger also builds something the whole network provably shares: a mind, grown one verified step per block, that no single party controls or can rewrite. And because the genesis block pays nobody, the only way NEURA has ever entered the world is that someone, somewhere, did the work — mining here is joining, and joining leaves a permanent mark on what the chain knows.

The honesty box. Neura is a real, working implementation — real ECDSA on secp256k1, a real UTXO ledger, real proof-of-work, and a genuinely learned (never stored) image — but it is a prototype, not a listed asset: coins have no market price, keys live unencrypted in your browser's localStorage, the network is tab-to-tab gossip, and the on-chain "AI" is a small neural net chosen so every node can verify every training step. Nothing here is investment advice. Read the full risk notes in README.md before trusting it with anything that matters.
