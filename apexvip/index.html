Questions

Good to know.

Where does ApexVIP operate?

Six cities today — London, Dubai, Paris, New York, Singapore and Zurich — covering every major airport plus the private aviation terminals. One account works across all of them, with your preferences carried city to city.

How does membership work?

Chauffeur membership is £199 a month and unlocks the booking desk: fixed fares, flight-tracked pickups and the full fleet — with the first 30 days complimentary for new members. The ApexVIP Concierge is a separate subscription from £49 a month across Silver, Gold and Black tiers.

What will my journey cost?

Every fare is fixed and shown before you confirm, inclusive of VAT — a typical Heathrow transfer is around £95. No surge pricing, ever.

Which vehicles can I choose?

The Mercedes-Benz S-Class for up to three guests, the V-Class for up to seven, and Rolls-Royce or speciality marques on request for weddings and special occasions.

How do airport pickups work?

We track your flight in real time and adjust automatically to delays. Your chauffeur meets you in arrivals, and waiting is complimentary for sixty minutes after you land.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — membership is monthly with no lock-in, cancellable in a tap from your account.
