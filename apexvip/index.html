Questions

Good to know.

How do I book a journey?

Every booking is made in the ApexVIP app — there's no booking on this website. Install the app from your browser (Share → Add to Home Screen on iPhone, Install app on Android), then ask ApexAI or tap out your journey. Your car, fare and chauffeur are confirmed in seconds.

Where does ApexVIP operate?

ApexVIP is live in London today — covering every major airport (Heathrow, Gatwick, City, Luton, Stansted) plus the private aviation terminals. Dubai is coming soon; reserve your place now to be among the first when it opens.

How does membership work?

Chauffeur membership is £199 a month and unlocks the booking desk: fixed fares, flight-tracked pickups and the full fleet — with the first 30 days complimentary for new members. The ApexVIP Concierge is a separate subscription from £49 a month across Silver, Gold and Black tiers.

What will my journey cost?

Every fare is fixed and shown before you confirm, inclusive of VAT — a typical Heathrow transfer is around £95. No surge pricing, ever.

Which vehicles can I choose?

The Mercedes-Benz S-Class for up to three guests, and the Mercedes-Benz V-Class for up to seven. Every car is chauffeur-driven, detailed before each journey and stocked with still water, Wi-Fi and charging.

How do airport pickups work?

We track your flight in real time and adjust automatically to delays. Your chauffeur meets you in arrivals, and waiting is complimentary for sixty minutes after you land.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — membership is monthly with no lock-in, cancellable in a tap from your account.

How do I become an ApexVIP chauffeur?

Register your interest in the form below and choose "Driving for ApexVIP". After licence, background and vehicle checks, you install the driver app, connect Stripe payouts and go online — keeping 80% of every fare, with no joining fee.
