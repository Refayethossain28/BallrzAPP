Cortex · a Proof-of-Learning blockchain

Miners don't burn hashes — they train a shared neural network. Every block is a signed, verifiable proof the model got smarter.

Each block trains from the tip's weights until the loss drops by at least the network's minImprovement, and mints MIND to you in proportion to the learning — so the money is earned by teaching the shared model. Verifying a block is one forward pass; the loss (and your reward) is recomputed, never trusted. Total supply is bounded by the knowledge the network can create, and stops growing once the model converges.
The shared task: classify a noisy XOR of two coordinates — impossible for a straight line. Dots are the dataset (two classes); the shaded field is the network's live decision boundary. Watch it bend as the chain learns.
