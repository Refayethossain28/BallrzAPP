What is this network training? A war-lethality model.

A shared neural network learning from real Correlates of War data: 2,324 genuine militarized confrontations between states. Given three signals available before the outcome — was there a mutual military build-up, how many events occurred, and the year — it predicts whether the confrontation turned lethal (any battle deaths) or not. Honest limits: three features and a research-era sample mean ~73% accuracy vs a ~58% baseline — a real, modest signal, not a crystal ball. This is a historical pattern learner ("does a standoff turn deadly?"), not live war prediction and not a market. The weights live in every block below — the chain is the model.
